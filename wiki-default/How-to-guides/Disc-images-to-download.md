# LaserDisc images to download

Disc images that have been captured with the DomesdayDuplicator (suitable for decoding with ld-decode) can be uploaded to the Internet Archive for preservation and storage.  Links to such disc images are included on this page.

When downloading from the IA site please ensure that you use either the FLAC or torrent option as IA may interpret (and transcode) the file as audio into mp3 or similar (lossy compression is not recommended for RF captures).

### The Domesday Project

These sets are the original 1986 BBC Domesday Project LaserDiscs.  Each set consists of two double-sided discs; one is National (A and B) and the other is Community (North and South).  The National discs have an additional marking which is shown below.  NP is “No Plus” (there is no marking), PP is “PlusPlus” (there are two plus symbols) and AK is “Asterisk”. ## Interactive Video

### The World, the UN and you

This archive contains 7 different discs of the same IV title captured on a calibrated LD-V4300D player.  This title aims to teach children about the UN.  Note that the software for this title has not been preserved (please get in touch if you have access to it):

[The World, the UN and you (7 disc sets)](https://archive.org/details/the-world-the-un-and-you-cav-pal)

### British Garden Birds

British Garden Birds is a disc developed by the BBC and is one of the first examples of video and data being combined in the LaserDisc format.  The disc contains teletext pages within the video and is widely considered to be the proof-of-concept for the later BBC Domesday project:

* [British Garden Birds Disc Set 1](https://archive.org/details/British_Garden_Birds-LaserDisc-DD86-Disc-Set-1)
* [British Garden Birds Disc Set 2](https://archive.org/details/British_Garden_Birds-LaserDisc-DD86-Disc-Set-2)
* [British Garden Birds Disc Set 3](https://archive.org/details/British_Garden_Birds-LaserDisc-DD86-Disc-Set-3)
* [British Garden Birds Disc Set 4](https://archive.org/details/British_Garden_Birds-LaserDisc-DD86-Disc-Set-4)

Details of the disc are available here: [British Garden Birds](https://www.domesday86.com/?page_id=3147).
